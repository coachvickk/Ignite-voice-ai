FAQ

Common Questions

Doesn't automation feel inauthentic to customers? +
Authenticity comes from being present when it matters. Voice AI ensures your customers are heard and supported even when you can't personally respond. Missing a call entirely — that's what feels inauthentic. Our AI sounds natural, responds intelligently, and makes customers feel taken care of from the first ring.
We've tried systems before and they didn't work. What's different? +
Most systems fail because they're designed for technology companies, not for how your business actually works. We start with your workflow, then build the system to support it — not the other way around. We also manage the system for you, so it doesn't rely on your team to maintain it.
My business is too complex for automation. +
Complex businesses benefit most from automation. We work with law firms, medical practices, and construction companies — businesses where every call is unique and high-stakes. Our Voice AI is trained on your specific services, pricing, team structure, and common caller questions.
I don't understand tech and don't want to manage it. +
You won't manage it. Our systems work quietly in the background while you focus on leadership and growth. We handle all setup, monitoring, and optimization. Your job is to show up to appointments — not to manage software.
Will this replace my staff? +
No. Voice AI handles the routine, repetitive calls — spam filtering, appointment scheduling, basic inquiries — so your team can focus on complex, high-value interactions that actually require a human. It empowers your staff, it doesn't replace them.
How long does it take to get set up? +
Most clients are live within 2–4 weeks of signing. We handle the entire buildout, testing, and training. You'll review and approve before anything goes live. Setup is smooth because we've done this many times — for roofing, legal, healthcare, and home services.
